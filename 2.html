Amed, Bali


Overall

Amed is a 14-km strip of fishing villages in Karangasem Regency on the east coast of Bali, Indonesia.[1] The area name Amed is taken from one of its seven villages. The other six are called Jemeluk, Bunutan, Lipah, Selang, Banyuning and Aas.
